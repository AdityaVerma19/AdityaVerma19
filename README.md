## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an **AI/ML Engineer** with a deep focus on **Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)** and **Large Language Model (LLM) orchestration**. Currently, I specialize in engineering end-to-end pipelines that extract, normalize, and structure complex domain-specific data. My work bridges the gap between raw data ingestion and semantic normalization, leveraging vision-language and MoE models to solve real-world industry challenges.

*   🏢 **Currently:** AI/ML Engineering Intern at **Novel Patterns**.
*   🚀 **Previous Experience:** Software Engineering Intern at **Stirring Minds** and **Woohl**.
*   ⚡ **Focus:** Document AI, PEFT/LoRA fine-tuning, RAG pipelines, and inference optimization.
*   🧠 **Fact:** I believe preserving full document context is the key to high-fidelity AI extraction.

---

## 🚀 Professional Experience

### **AI/ML Engineering Intern | Novel Patterns**
*   **Intelligent Financial Extraction:** Engineered production-grade AI pipelines to extract structured information (Balance Sheets, Profit & Loss statements, and notes) from complex financial PDFs, mapping them to standardized schemas.
*   **Context-Aware Processing:** Architected a data engineering approach that processes full-context PDFs rather than sectioned pages, significantly improving the model's ability to retain and understand global document context.
*   **Automated Ingestion Systems:** Built robust dataset generation and batch-processing scripts using `fitz` (PyMuPDF), `pytesseract`, and `pandas` to systematically categorize document images for training and inference.
*   **Semantic Matching & Alias Generation:** Investigated and deployed **Google Gemma** models to automate the updating of financial terminology in Excel sheets, developing zero-shot strategies for alias generation and new term categorization.
*   **Model Optimization:** Fine-tuned the **NuExtract** model utilizing **LoRA** and **PEFT** configurations for highly accurate data extraction. Implemented evaluation steps using the **OmniDocBench** framework.

### **Software Engineering Intern | Stirring Minds & Woohl**
*   **Scalable AI Solutions:** Architected RAG-based chatbots and integrated scalable full-stack solutions.
*   **End-to-End Deployment:** Built and deployed robust applications, including an AI-powered e-commerce platform and diagnostic machine learning systems.
