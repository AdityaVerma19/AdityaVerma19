## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an **AI/ML Engineer** currently building cutting-edge solutions at **Novel Patterns**. I specialize in Document AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and developing robust pipelines that extract, normalize, and structure complex domain-specific data. My core focus lies at the intersection of natural language processing and financial data extraction.

*   🔭 I’m currently working on **Document Intelligence** and advanced OCR parsing pipelines.
*   🧠 I’m deeply exploring **Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT/LoRA)** and semantic matching.
*   ⚡ Fact: I believe preserving full document context is the key to high-fidelity AI extraction.

---

## 🚀 Professional Experience & Projects

### 🧠 Document AI & Pipeline Engineering
*   **Intelligent Financial Extraction:** Engineered an end-to-end AI pipeline to extract structured information (Balance Sheets, Profit & Loss statements, and financial notes) from complex, unstructured financial PDFs and map them to standardized schemas.
*   **Context-Aware Processing:** Architected a data engineering approach that processes full-context PDFs rather than sectioned pages, significantly improving the model's ability to retain and understand global document context.
*   **Automated Ingestion Systems:** Built robust dataset generation and batch-processing scripts using `fitz` (PyMuPDF), `pytesseract`, and `pandas` to systematically categorize document images for training and inference.

### ⚙️ Large Language Model (LLM) Optimization
*   **Model Fine-Tuning:** Fine-tuned the **NuExtract** model utilizing **LoRA** and **PEFT** configurations for highly accurate, structure-aware data extraction from complex balance sheets.
*   **Benchmarking & Evaluation:** Implemented rigorous model evaluation frameworks, utilizing **OmniDocBench** to test and validate the performance of fine-tuned models on document intelligence tasks.
*   **Semantic Matching Strategies:** Investigated and deployed **Google Gemma** models to automate the updating of financial terminology in Excel sheets, successfully developing zero-shot strategies for alias generation and new term categorization.
